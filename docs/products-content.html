Cloud Unit Solutions

Heat Reuse - Ideal for situations where continuous heat is required year-round. For example swimming pool or aquatic ponds.

Heat storage - The buffer tank can store heat, ready to be used when extra heat is needed. Ideal for offices, residential housing, industrial processes or laundry facilities. The Cloud Unit can be utilized for heating both tap water and providing radiant floor heating.

Cooling Towers - By incorporating the cooling towers, we can efficiently dissipate heat that isn't reused.

Cloud Unit Cassette flavors

A Cloud Unit has two casettes. Each casette can has one of the follwing flavors: compute, storage or AI.
